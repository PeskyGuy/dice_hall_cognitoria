## Personality

Absolute alcoholic, very jovial and can be profound on occassion.

### Ideals

Vessel believes in finding purpose and beating up bad guys.

### Flaws & Fears

Their alcoholic tendencies can get them, even if resistant to becoming drunk they still can and that's bad.

### Motivations

To save the world! To vanquish the evil that plagues Terra! Defeat the woman mentioned in the taverns in a drinking contest.

## Background

Vessel was birthed out of a tree, appearing in front of most of the party in [[Compendia Mundorum/Campaigns/Terra Ultima\|Terra Ultima]]. Now they adventure along side them.

### Early Life

Not much was known about Vessel, other than claiming he was an agent of the Goddess he serves.

### Defining Moments

Vessel drank so much alcohol it fused with their current form.
