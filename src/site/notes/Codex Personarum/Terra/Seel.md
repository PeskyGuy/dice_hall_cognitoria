## Appearance

Looks like a small blob of honey, they wear a poncho and cowboy hat with a golden four-leaf clover pin. They have big white eyes and a tiny smile.

---

## Personality

Loves their Gambler's Blade dearly, but let it go before they died fighting their father. Soul loves helping in whatever way he can. He is very naive at times and their lack of the concept of money leaves them broke for the most part.

### Ideals

Soul, believed in serving a purpose and forming bonds stronger than family.

### Flaws & Fears

They are a materialist without the concept of money and its value and the fact their naivety can get the better of them.

### Motivations

To save the world! To vanquish the evil that plagues Terra!

---

## Background

Soul was a piece of the Primordial Ooze, the Architect of Life. Now blessed by the Goddess of Luck, they have changed for the better and sent to defeat the great evil.

### Early Life

Not much was known about Soul, other than claiming he was an agent of the Goddess he serves.

### Defining Moments

Soul alongside their party created the unbreakable cipher, with the rewards of the Gambler's Blade and a lot of platinum.

Soul, became Seel and fulfilled their purpose of saving the world.
