## Appearance

Dark skin, Brown hair, and Green eyes. A very beautiful woman, who prefers lighter clothing to allow for more freedom of movement. She's usually in dancer attire and constantly has a red nose.

---

## Personality

Loves drinking more than life, is practically drunk 24/7. When drunk, she usually dances a lot. When not drunk, she is extremely shy, barely speaking to anyone outside of her sister in a very meek voice.

### Ideals

"live and let live" is Shelly's motto in life. She likes to believe in the goodness of people.

### Flaws & Fears

Would probably do something very dangerous to try a new drink. Once she starts drinking, she doesn't know how to stop until she runs out.

### Motivations

Shelly, alongside her sister, seeks to find their father. During her downtime, she indulges herself by finding the strongest drinks around and competing in drinking competitions. Saving the world is a sweet bonus.

---

## Background

Shelly is one of a pair of twins, and the daughter of a God. Which God, that is something she aims to find out.

### Early Life

Shelly lived with her grandma in the country side after her mother passed away. When she came of age, she went out to the world to find her father, with only a letter that he left her as her only clue of his whereabouts.

### Defining Moments

Obtaining and transferring the soul of her sister into her very own body with the help of her friend Remiel.

Annoying the God of the world with her ability to ignore enemies' engagement and attacks of opportunity.
