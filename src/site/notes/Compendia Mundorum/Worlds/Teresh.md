The world where the campaigns Microcosm and Veilpiercer take place.

## General Info

## Ohsa

Ohsa is a coastal nation of approximately 4 billion people located along the Sapphire Sea. The capital city of Vyrodin houses 600 million residents and serves as the center of government, commerce, and the Mass Arcane Web network.

The country was founded in 1935 A.U. (After Union) following the Civil Centennial War, a devastating 100-year conflict between magic-focused and technology-focused factions. The war ended when six legendary heroes defeated the tyrant Vaxara and established The Union, a government built on combining magic and technology.

In 1970 A.U., five of the six heroes mysteriously disappeared. The Church of Embrace assumed administrative control and has governed for the past 35 years. The current year is 2005 A.U.

---
## Vyrodin

Vyrodin was built on the ruins of Vaxhold fortress where the final battle of the war took place. The city is divided into eight major districts:

**Holy District** - Government center and Church headquarters. The safest and most affluent area.

**Free District** - Originally a refugee settlement, now known for high crime rates and underground markets.

**Ostra Gate** - Primarily residential neighborhoods with parks and community spaces.

**Lezo's Gate** - Industrial zone with factories and artificer workshops.

**Grezer's Path** - Working-class district with strong community organizations despite economic challenges.

**Docks District** - Major shipping and trade hub along the coast.

**The Undercity** - Extensive tunnel network beneath the city operating largely outside official oversight.

**Outer Sprawl** - Rapidly expanding settlements beyond traditional city boundaries.
